Rules


#Who may participate?

The competition is open to undergraduate students, and gradaute students of all nationalities. Participants can be individually or in maximum 3 people group.


#How to submit my work? (CLOSED)

1. Fill the register form below. Please fill the information of the representative if you are a team.
2. You will recieve an email with a formal register form. Please fill the form and send it back to finish registration.
3. You are successfully registered once you recieve confirmation email.


#Regulation of the landing page

The landing page is limited within one page.
We will need you to provide source code, as a zip file and email us .
Websites should be original work, have to be live for the duration of the judging period and should comply with all Terms and Conditions laid out by the Visionbaker team.


#What will be judeged?

Design 20%, programimg skill 50%, RWD + cross platform 20%
